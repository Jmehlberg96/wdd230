Discover Green Valley

Welcome to Green Valley; established in 1976. We are home to 50,0259 people who feel we are the most beautiful city in the southwest. We have the honor of entertaining millions of tourists annually due to the variety of attractions that we offer year round. Whether you like to hike, climb, golf, off-road, bike, or attend one of our many concerts and theater attractions, we have something for you.

Mountain Biking

Green Valley is connected by miles of bike trails that weave in and out of the city. You will find hundreds of different hiking and bike trails to choose from that take you through some of the most beautiful scenery around. Don't have a bike? We got you covered with one of our many rental bike shops available.

Rock Climbing / Hiking

If rock climbing is you scene then we have got you covered. Choose from a list of many great mountains that individuals from all over the world come to climb. There are places for beginners and those that like to live on the edge. If you need a guide then just visit one of the outdoor activity business and they will hook you up.

Shopping

We are not all about the outdoors. Shopping is high on the list for visitors as well. Green Valley has some of the most unique and fun shops around. If you need it, we have it in one of our many shops that line main street and surrounding areas. You will not be disappointed by all the beautiful things you will find.

National Parks

Just outside of Green Valley is the beautiful Zions National Park where thousand of people all over the world travel to see the amazing views and hike some of the most beautiful trails in the world. With its grand peaks and amazing waterfalls you won't be disappointed in the least.

Entertainment

If you love concerts and plays then our Tuacahn theater is the place to be. With events every week of the year, there is something for everyone. Tuacahn host famous musicians and artist along with community plays. You will always have something to see here.

Farmers Market

Do you love fresh fruits and vegetables straight from the farm? Then our farmers market is the place to be. Every Saturday morning you will find local farmers selling some beautiful and fresh produce along with locally grown honey and other fresh items that you must try.

Golfing

Home to the famous Green Valley Golf Resort, where we host the PGA tour every other year. Our beautiful greens and difficult course make it a challenge for even the pro golfers; but don't let that intimidate you. Our smaller courses around our city are just as beautiful and fun and perfect for the novice golfer.

Off-Roading

The big boulders and petrified sand dunes that are on the outskirts of Green Valley play host to the rock crawlers from all over the south west. If you have a 4 x 4 and love to drive it on the edge, then we are the place to visit. Join hundreds of off-road vehicles every other weekend as they crawl across the landscape.

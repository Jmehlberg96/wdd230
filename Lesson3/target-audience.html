Target Audience

The Green Valley Chamber of Commerce websites target audiences are consumers looking for quality businesses in their community that they can support and receive quality products and services while understanding that as they support local businesses, they are building up their community. It is also for local businesses who want to grow their business in our community, provide a quality service to customers, advertise with other reputable companies who share their same goals, and increase their networking opportunities. If you are a tourist and are looking for a place to stay, eat and play and enjoy yourself while staying in our town, all you have to do is look up our website and you will be directed to many businesses that will be able to fulfill you needs. Even the local public can use our website to find information on current events and activities for the whole family to participate in.

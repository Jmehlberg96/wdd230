About Us

Bountiful Foods was founded in 1998 by a group of health-conscious entrepreneurs who saw a need for high-quality, organic fruit and fruit drinks in their community. They started out by selling their products at local farmers markets, but soon expanded to open their first brick and mortar store in 2001.

From the beginning, Bountiful Foods has been committed to using only the freshest, locally sourced, organic ingredients in their products. They believe that this not only produces the best-tasting fruit and drinks but also supports local farmers and promotes sustainable agriculture.

Over the years, Bountiful Foods has gained a loyal following of customers who appreciate their commitment to quality and sustainability. In 2010, they opened their first food truck, which allowed them to reach even more customers at festivals and events around the region.

Bountiful Foods has also been recognized for their philanthropic efforts. In 2015, they started a program called "Fruit for All" which donates a portion of their profits to local food banks and community organizations that support access to healthy food options.

Today, Bountiful Foods is a well-established brand with a reputation for quality, sustainability, and community involvement. They continue to grow and innovate, introducing new fruit varieties and custom-made drinks to their loyal customers.

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Bountiful Foods is to provide fresh, locally grown, and organic fruits and produce to its customers. Additionally, the company offers made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags to promote healthy eating and convenience. The functional specifications for the company include selling directly from local farmers, using some national and international suppliers, managing orders through their storefront, food trucks, phone, and website, and catering to both local and tourist populations in the coastal areas. The industry that Bountiful Foods operates in is the food and beverage industry, with a specific focus on healthy, organic, and locally sourced products. As for viable goals, Bountiful Foods objective is to increase sales, expand our product offerings, and continuing to build our brand reputation for providing fresh and healthy food options.
